## Customer Units

In Agile, Customer units is team  represent customers/market targetted by a product. 

Customer Unit's Job :
- Vision product
- Road Map of Product
- Creating and Maintaining Product
- Interviewing Customer
- Determine target market

Example Person / Roles :
->Product Managers
->Sales 
->Marketing 
->End User
